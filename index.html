What is an SOS Badge?

Every one of us has a wish to protect and keep our loved ones safe at all times; however, until now that has been challenging. With that in mind, VesCo Solutions created a device to put our minds at ease by providing a fast and accurate way to inform the proper authorities to come help our loved ones based on their emergency. All that is done with the press of a button, using our SOS Cards. The SOS Card is a wearable. It can be used by teachers, employees, students, neighborhood watches, and many other possible users, to send a message with an exact location to a security office or across the entire facility - providing the exact spot where the help should go. The SOS message can be sent to security systems for an automatic activation of alarms and / or to a remote location inside or outside the premises to ensure the reach of proper help.

What does it do?

  • 1- It sends an immediate SOS notification from our wearable button or SafeKeeper™ mobile app via our secure portal.
  • 2- SOS messages have real-time location to show police / first responders exactly where and when the button press came from. The messages also include user profile information
  • 3- The SOS card is compact, easy to carry, and can be used as a school ID, work badge, and cell phone cover while still opening locks and providing a life line for emergencies
  • 4- Within a click, you can lock down the premises, activate alerts, and send notifications to mobile devices in the network warning registered users.
  • 5- SOS activity is logged and responses reviewed utilizing video footage to ensure quality control and proactive training to First Responders and Control Center personnel.
  • 6- View Live Video coverage from the SafeKeeper app or use security system throughout the premises to view or capture emergencies. All footage is saved under the user’s profile on SafeKeeper.

How does it work?

  • 1- Emergency is identified and user presses the button
  • 2- Notification is received by Control Center, and First Responders are informed and dispatched
  • 3- Security system, including available video coverage, is activated to follow the events
  • 4-All responders are able to assess the situation and take best procedures until arriving at the location sent by the card or app
